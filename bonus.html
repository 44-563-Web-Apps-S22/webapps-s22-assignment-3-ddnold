The Nashville Neutralizer

The Nashville Neutralizer is the mascott for Nashvilles college football team. He is an orange dart frog with black spots, and is always there with Nashville neutralizes another competitor.

  1. He sometime will run instead of jumping everywhere
  2. Nashville loves to shoot a fake dart out of him during the pregame
  3. When Nashville wins, it's a tradition that the opposing mascott acts as though touching the dartfrog hurt them.
