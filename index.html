I like to buy food from Black Pony Brewing

The Black Pony Brewing Co. is located on the square in maryville. It's located right next door to the courthouse, license bureau, and the best bar in town, The Pub.

Travel

The closest airport to the town of Maryville is the "NW Missouri Regional Airport-Evu". In order to get to Black Pony Brewery from there you will need to do the following:

  1. Take Hawk Rd. to Hwy 46.
  2. Take Hwy 46 or 1st st. into town and take a left on Main st.
  3. Follow Main st. 3 blocks North.
    • Your destination is on your right on the corner of 4th and Main.

If you get a chance to go, I recommend trying the following:

  • Triple Crown Melt
  • Chicken Parm
  • Any flatbread pizza
  • Also try one of my favorite beers...
    • 50 Shades of Hay
    • The Big Brown
    • The American Pharaoh
